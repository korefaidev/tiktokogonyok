Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Streak Keeper handles information when you use the Android application.

1. Information we store locally

Streak Keeper stores app settings locally on your device, including selected chats, message templates, scheduling preferences, active days, streak status, language preference, and connection status.

2. TikTok Login Kit

If you choose to connect TikTok, the app may use TikTok Login Kit with the user.info.basic scope to authenticate your account and show that the account is connected. Any authorization flow is handled through TikTok's official login experience.

3. No sale of personal information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

4. Data sharing

Streak Keeper does not share locally stored settings with third parties unless you configure an authorized backend integration or where required by law.

5. Notifications

The app may send local notifications when a scheduled action fails. Notification permission can be managed in Android system settings.

6. Data retention and deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. You can disconnect TikTok within the app to remove stored connection data.

7. Security

We use local device storage for app settings. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and you are responsible for keeping your device secure.

8. Children's privacy

Streak Keeper is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law or TikTok's terms. Do not use the app if you are not allowed to use TikTok or similar services in your region.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: streakkeeper@example.com
